The Sci-Hub project hosts the internet's largest collection of research papers and other scientific educational resources regardless of artificially restrictive copyright laws that effectively slow down the progress of science in human society. As of today, the Sci-Hub page still lacks the option to browse and search the Sci-Hub database without a DOI identifier. We provide this search functionality for Sci-Hub.
